    ABOUT ME

    Who am I ?

    I am someone who wants to start his career in Software Engineering as best as one could, being all about making the best possible exchange of value between me and those who tutor me. I grew up in a beautiful country named Romania, but out of professional, peace of mind and studying reasons I moved to Dublin on 13.th of August 2021.

    Why Software Engineering?

    My first encounter with understanding software felt like struggling to open a door in the dark and suddenly hearing the noise of the key sliding perfectly into the lock.

    I instantly felt in love with the power of abstraction, and I realised I could be part of something that can solve the problems of a billion people while developing myself in the proccess.

    My current goals

    I. to be all the time surrounded by smarter people than me

    II. to build working solutions to the most difficult problems among the brightest minds.

    III. to enjoy myself in the proccess and create high-quality interractions
